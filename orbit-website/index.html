Capabilities

Everything a copilot should do —
and most it never could.

Orbit doesn't live in a tab. It lives on your desktop, watching, reading, writing, and acting.

Screen-aware context

Orbit captures your screen — or just a region you draw — and reasons over what it sees. Worksheets, stack traces, designs, dashboards. No copy-paste.

✎

Real file edits

Surgical patch_file diffs, full writes, moves, deletes — sandboxed to your workspace with an undo timeline.

Runs commands

Frequently asked.

Everything you might want to know before launch.

How do I activate a plan?
There's no self-serve checkout yet. Pick a plan and hit Contact M4sh3r — you'll be set up, usually within a day.
Is my data private?
Voice dictation runs on a local Whisper model on your machine — nothing is uploaded. Chat requests go to the Voyager models to generate responses; your files stay on your device unless you ask Orbit to act on them.
What's the difference between the plans?
Higher tiers unlock more capable Voyager models, Agents mode, background agents, desktop automation, and higher daily limits. See the comparison table above.
What does the daily message limit mean?
It's the number of AI requests per day on your plan (50 / 200 / 600 / unlimited). Read-only actions and local voice don't count against it. The counter resets every day.
Which platforms are supported?
Orbit is a Windows 10/11 desktop app (Electron). macOS and Linux are on the roadmap.
Can I switch or cancel plans?
Yes — message M4sh3r anytime to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel. Changes apply from your next cycle.
